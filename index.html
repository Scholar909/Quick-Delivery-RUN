Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Quick Delivery?
Quick Delivery is a fast, reliable, and trustworthy delivery service designed to make your everyday life easier. Whether it’s groceries, food, or important packages, we deliver with speed and integrity.

2. How does Quick Delivery work?
Simply place your order through our app, choose your delivery option, and track your package in real-time. Our riders handle the rest—quickly and safely.

3. What makes Quick Delivery different from others?
We don’t just move packages—we deliver trust. Our focus is on honesty, transparency, and ensuring that your items arrive safely and on time, every time.

4. Where does Quick Delivery operate?
We’re starting with key cities and expanding rapidly. Keep an eye out—we might be in your area sooner than you think.

5. How fast can I expect my delivery?
Delivery times vary based on distance and order type, but we pride ourselves on being as quick as possible without ever compromising safety or quality.

6. Is my package safe with Quick Delivery?
Absolutely. We treat every item with care and accountability. From secure packaging to real-time tracking, we ensure your delivery is handled responsibly.

7. What can I send with Quick Delivery?
From everyday essentials to urgent packages, we’ve got you covered. (Restricted items like hazardous goods or illegal products are not permitted.)

8. How do I contact Quick Delivery if I have an issue?
You can reach us directly through the app’s support feature, by email, or through our customer care line. We’re always here to help.
