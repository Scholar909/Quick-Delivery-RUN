Quick Delivery

Policies

1. Respect & Conduct

  • Everyone (customers and employees) must treat each other with respect.
  • Harassment, threats, or verbal abuse are not tolerated.

2. Service Commitment

  • We aim to deliver within agreed timelines, except in situations beyond our control (e.g., traffic, weather, accidents).
  • Fragile or sensitive items must be clearly labeled; staff must handle them with extra care.

3. Payment

  • Customers must pay before delivery unless a credit arrangement is made.
  • Employees must submit all collected payments daily.

4. Privacy

  • Customer information (address, phone number, etc.) will only be used for delivery.
  • Employees must never share customer or company data outside Quick Delivery.

5. Safety

  • Customers must not request unsafe or illegal deliveries.
  • Any accidents must be reported immediately to management.

6. Rewards & Discipline

  • Loyal customers may receive discounts or rewards.
  • Hardworking employees may receive bonuses or recognition.
  • Violations by employees or customers can result in penalties, suspension, or blacklisting.

7. Growth & Improvement

  • Quick Delivery is committed to improving its services.
  • Customers and employees will be informed when we expand, add new services, or upgrade systems.
